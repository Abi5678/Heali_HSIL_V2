Features

Everything You Need, One Conversation Away

From medication reminders to emergency detection, Heali covers every dimension of your health journey.

Voice Guardian

Talk to your AI health companion naturally. Gemini Live powers real-time voice conversations that understand context and emotion.

Never Miss a Dose

Smart medication reminders via push notifications and Twilio voice calls. Press 1 when taken, 2 to snooze. Tracks adherence over time.

Speak Your Language

Fluent in English, Hindi, Kannada, and Spanish. Each language comes with a unique companion persona and voice.

Smart Health Dashboard

Track blood pressure, heart rate, glucose, SpO2, and weight. See 7-day trends, risk scores, and daily health digests at a glance.

Pill Verification

Point your camera at any pill. Heali identifies it and verifies it against your prescription using AI vision. No more guessing.

Food & Nutrition

Snap a photo of your meal. Heali instantly breaks down calories, protein, carbs, and fat. Track your daily nutrition targets.

Prescription Scanner

Scan prescriptions and lab reports with your camera. Heali extracts medications, dosages, test results, and flags drug interactions.

Family Care Network

Link family members with a simple code. Caregivers see adherence scores, vital trends, and receive RED/AMBER/GREEN safety alerts.

Book a Doctor

Find nearby hospitals by specialty and distance. Book appointments with voice commands. Auto-generates clinical briefs for your doctor.

Exercise & Wellness

Guided breathing, stretching, and cool-down sessions with real-time posture coaching via your camera. Your personal wellness coach.

Live Medical Interpreter

Real-time voice translation between you and your doctor. Speak Hindi, hear English. Maintains exact medical terminology.

Emergency Detection

Automatic detection of critical symptoms like chest pain or severe bleeding. Instantly alerts your family and activates emergency protocol.
